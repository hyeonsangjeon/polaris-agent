# Polaris Agent Desktop

## Register

product

## Users

Operators running durable local or private-network agent workloads who need to inspect work, curate prompt memory, schedule recurring tasks, and resolve delivery uncertainty without exposing credentials.

## Product Purpose

Provide a trustworthy control plane for agent runs and their durable records. Success means operators can understand current state, make explicit safety decisions, and recover interrupted work without leaving the desktop console.

## Brand Personality

Calm, exact, operational. The interface should feel dependable under incident pressure and stay out of the operator's way.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative AI dashboards, opaque automation, consumer-chat styling, secret-bearing diagnostics, and ambiguous destructive actions.

## Design Principles

- Make durable state and provenance legible.
- Require explicit operator intent for uncertain or destructive actions.
- Prefer familiar controls and dense, scan-friendly information.
- Explain safety defaults at the point of action.
- Keep credentials and secret values behind the Rust boundary.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA with semantic headings and forms, visible keyboard focus, non-color state labels, responsive navigation, and reduced-motion support.
